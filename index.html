Baseline Benefits helps you understand and use your health benefits. It's not tax, legal, or medical advice. HSA eligibility depends on your plan and IRS rules, and prices are estimates. We never tell you to change your medication.

  1. Many everyday items are HSA/FSA-eligible, including over-the-counter drugs like aspirin (no prescription needed since the 2020 CARES Act) and sunscreen (SPF 15+, broad-spectrum). See IRS Publication 502 and Fidelity's eligible-expenses list. Eligibility varies by plan.
  2. Paying a discounted cash price (for example, with GoodRx) generally does not count toward your insurance deductible, while an insurance copay does. See GoodRx Support.
