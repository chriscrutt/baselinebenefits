01 · The problem

Healthcare runs on paperwork, not software.

Americans spend more on healthcare than almost anything else, yet manage it with none of the tools they expect for the rest of their money.

  • Most people can't say what their HSA covers, so tax-free money sits unused.
  • Same drug, same pharmacy: a $29 copay when the cash price is $12. Nobody tells you.
  • Reimbursements go unfiled because the paperwork is miserable.
  • Medical bills hide costly errors, and no tool checks them for you.

Prices illustrative; discount cash prices often beat copays (GoodRx).

02 · Why now

Three shifts made this possible.

The tools to fix healthcare's money problem only recently came together.

AI

People expect apps to do the work for them now, not just show information.

Price transparency

New rules finally made healthcare pricing data usable.

Connected finance

Open banking and digital wallets let apps link your accounts and move money for you.

03 · Solution

Baseline Benefits. The AI financial OS for healthcare.

It connects your HSA, insurance, pharmacy, and bank, then works in the background to find what's eligible, compare every price, file reimbursements, time your deductible, and forecast what's next.

Healthcare money decisions become automatic.

07 · Business model

Free for you. Paid by the money we move.

Free for the people who use it. We earn from pharmacies, merchants, and card rails, plus a cut of the money we recover, and none of it depends on employer or health-plan budgets.

  • Prescription discount transaction fees
  • Fees from merchants who accept HSA / FSA
  • Interchange + interest on HSA balances
  • Pharma copay & assistance programs
  • A cut of recovered reimbursements, earned only when you get paid
  • Employer & health-plan contracts (expansion)

Fees like these are about 82% of GoodRx revenue.
