01 · The problem

Healthcare runs on paperwork, not software.

Americans spend more on healthcare than almost anything else, yet manage it with none of the tools they expect for the rest of their money.

  • People don't know what their HSA actually covers.
  • Thousands in reimbursements go unclaimed.
  • Prescription prices swing wildly for the same drug.
  • Medical bills hide costly errors.
  • Insurance decisions are guesswork.
  • No one gives you a straight, personal answer.
02 · Why now

Three shifts made this possible.

The tools to fix healthcare's money problem only recently came together.

AI

People now expect a proactive assistant to handle the hard stuff.

Price transparency

New rules finally made healthcare pricing data usable.

Connected finance

Open banking and digital wallets make automation real.

03 · Solution

Baseline. The AI financial OS for healthcare.

It connects your HSA, insurance, pharmacy, and bank, then works in the background to find what's eligible, compare every price, file reimbursements, time your deductible, and forecast what's next.

Healthcare money decisions become automatic.

07 · Business model

Free for you. Paid by the money we move.

Baseline is free for the people who use it. We earn from the savings and transactions we create, never from your pocket.

  • Prescription discount transaction fees
  • HSA / FSA "unlock" fees from merchants
  • Interchange + interest on HSA balances
  • Pharma copay & assistance programs
  • A small cut of reimbursements we recover
  • Employer & health-plan contracts (expansion)

It doesn't depend on payer budgets.
